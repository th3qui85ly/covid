Things To Know

A major of the things should be kept in mind by one, to stay protected from the Novel Corona Virus (COVID19).

  • No person should touch their face, eyes, nose, ears, mouth, uneccessarily. It causes the fast spread of the bacteria wholeover your body.

  • One should avoid all type of social gatherings (like at any religious place, lounges, bars, hotels, restaurants, transport stations, markets, malls, supermarkets, etc). This leads to the easy flow of virus from one person to another by the physical contacts.

    • Corona Virus - Dos and Don'ts

  • Wash your hands for atleast 20 second, for every 1-2 hour. Use antiseptic soaps, or handwash to clean your hands. Use alcohol based sanitizers for keeping your hands bacteria-free.

  • Maintain a distance of atleast 6ft.(~2 metres) in social contact. Avoid having any physical contact or gestures.

  • Avoid going outside your home during this crucial time of pandemic. Stay at home, quarantine yourself, avoid entrance of anything in your home without being sanitised.
